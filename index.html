*NEW for 2022*


This summer, in addition to speed/agility development, Tiger Training will also include strength training for our 5th-8th grade Future Tigers! As part of this program, our certified strength coach will utilize our state-of-the-art Blue Valley High School weight room facilities to teach movements Tiger Training athletes will use both in athletics and for the rest of their lives.


Athletes will have the option to enroll in either the Speed Program (Mon/Wed), the Strength Program (Tues/Thurs), or they can enroll in the Complete Tiger Training Program (M/T/W/Th).


Tiger Training is a 6 week, total sports performance program specifically designed for this age group's physical development levels. Both the Speed Program and the Strength Program are led by Blue Valley coaches and will be supervised by the BVHS Certified Athletic Trainer, Caitlin Truhe, MHPE, LAT, ATC. We are so excited to train our future Tigers!


All sessions will start on the turf at BVHS Stadium 6001 W. 159th St. Overland Park, KS 66085

Going to miss some days?


Athletes can miss up to 4 pre-selected days when you register. This allows for family vacations and other commitments. For every pre-selected absence, $10 is reduced from your total fee.
